# Shopify Catalog Health Pre-MVP Context

## What We Are Building

We are validating a Shopify-focused micro-SaaS idea before building the full Shopify app.

The product is a catalog health checker for Shopify stores. It helps merchants find and fix weak product pages, missing SEO fields, poor product descriptions, missing image alt text, duplicate/weak titles, and messy product data.

The first version is not a real Shopify app yet. It is a pre-MVP validation package designed to test whether store owners care enough to pay.

## Current Strategy

Do not build the full Shopify app first.

Instead:

1. Build a simple landing page.
2. Create realistic mock screenshots.
3. Offer a free 10-product catalog audit.
4. Message 100 Shopify store owners in one niche.
5. Ask whether they would pay $19-$49/month to fix the issues in bulk.
6. Build the MVP only if 5-10 merchants show serious interest.

## Proposed Product Positioning

Working positioning:

> Find and fix weak Shopify product pages before they cost you sales.

The offer:

> Get a free 10-product catalog audit for your Shopify store.

The future paid app:

> A Shopify app that scans your catalog, finds product content and SEO issues, suggests fixes with AI, lets you preview changes, and applies approved updates in bulk.

## First Target Niche

Recommended first niche:

Fashion/apparel Shopify stores.

Reason:

- Many products need descriptive copy.
- Product pages are visual and easy to audit publicly.
- SEO titles, descriptions, and image alt text matter.
- Store owners are often active on Instagram and email.

## MVP Validation Metrics

For 100 personalized outreach messages:

- 20+ replies
- 10+ free audits requested
- 5+ merchants say they would pay or ask when it is ready
- 2-3 merchants agree to join beta

If these numbers are weak, adjust the niche, message, or offer before building the actual app.

## Future Shopify App Concept

The real MVP, after validation, would be a Shopify embedded app.

Core features:

- Shopify OAuth install flow
- Read products from Shopify
- Product issue dashboard
- Single product optimization
- Bulk optimization for selected products
- Brand voice settings
- Preview before applying changes
- Push approved updates back to Shopify
- Monthly usage limits
- Shopify subscription billing

Pricing hypothesis:

- Starter: $9-$19/month
- Growth: $29-$49/month
- Pro: $79/month+

## Important Decision

At this stage, avoid spending weeks building the full app. The goal is market signal, not software completeness.
