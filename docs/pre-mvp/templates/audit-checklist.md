## Product Selection

Choose 10 public product pages.

Prefer:

- Best sellers if visible
- New arrivals
- Products with obvious weak descriptions
- Products from one important collection
- A mix of simple and variant-heavy products

## Per-Product Checks

For each product, record:

- Product name
- Product URL
- Product category
- Current title
- Current description
- Visible price
- Variant labels
- URL slug
- Visible image context
- Page title/meta description if visible

Assess:

- Is the title clear and descriptive?
- Is the description complete enough to answer buyer questions?
- Does the page communicate material, fit, size, care, usage, or benefit?
- Does the product need a stronger SEO title or meta description?
- Are images likely missing useful alt text?
- Is the URL slug clean and readable?
- Are variant names clear?
- Is there enough trust/conversion context?

Severity:

- High: likely affects search, trust, or buying decision
- Medium: useful improvement but not urgent
- Low: polish or consistency issue

## AI Helper Prompt

```text
You are helping audit Shopify product pages for catalog quality, SEO, and conversion clarity. Review the provided product title, description, URL, visible image context, and category. Identify issues, rate severity, and suggest improved title, description, and meta description. Keep suggestions commercially realistic and avoid exaggerated claims.
```

## Quality Notes

- Do not claim access to private Shopify admin data.
- Do not promise ranking increases.
- Do not exaggerate medical, beauty, or performance claims.
- Keep recommendations practical and merchant-friendly.
- Always frame this as a sample audit, not a complete store scan.
